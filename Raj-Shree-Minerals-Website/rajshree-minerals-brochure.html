About Us

Three Decades of Excellence in Mineral Manufacturing

Welcome to Rajshree Mineral

Rajshree Minerals started serving the nation in the year 1994, since then we are successfully meeting the demands of our esteemed patrons.

We are one of the quality manufacturer of hydrated lime, quick lime and limestone in our highly well equipped plants. We Rajshree Minerals located in village ransigaon, teh. Bilara, distt. Jodhpur.

We procured best quality of lime stone to produce good quality of quick lime. Quality, commitment and punctuality is our companies policy. Installed capacity of 60 MT of hydrated lime and quick lime per day.

Today, we are recognized as one of the reliable manufacturer and supplier of natural minerals in the country.
